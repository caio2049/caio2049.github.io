Number 8: Impulsiveness, success, luck, charisma, and abundance

People with Kabbalah number 8 love achievements and success. These people look for abundance and cyclic karmic nature. To these folks, life runs on the fact that what goes around comes around. These people believe that their sole strength is their charisma and never put a step back in putting in effort and hard work. Also, they love to achieve in their life. Therefore, one can say that they focus on material matters as well.
