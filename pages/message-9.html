Number 9: Fortune, service, selflessness, and completion

Natives with Kabbalah number 9 are commitment-loving people. They live their life with some purpose and focus on big goals. Moreover, these natives believe in completion. However, they never lack in making sacrifices for others. They love to spend time helping others, but on the other hand, they also have an ego. Therefore, these people take no time in making detachments. They also focus on happiness, and generosity is their second name.
